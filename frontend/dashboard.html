Quick guide to AI DocMaster

How do I merge PDFs?
Go to PDF Tools → Merge PDFs, drag and drop or select multiple PDF files, then click "Merge Now".
How does OCR work?
Upload an image (PNG/JPG) or scanned PDF under OCR Extract. The system uses Tesseract to extract text.
What is Chat with PDF?
Upload a PDF and ask questions about its content using Google Gemini AI for intelligent answers.
How is my data stored?
Files are stored locally on the server. Your account is secured with JWT authentication and bcrypt password hashing.

Need more help? Contact us at support@aidocmaster.com
